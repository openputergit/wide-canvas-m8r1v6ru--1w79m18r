Energy Conservation: The 4R's

Learn about the 4R's of Energy Conservation

Discover how to save energy and protect our environment through Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, and Recover

Reduce

Minimize energy consumption by turning off unused appliances and using energy-efficient devices.

Reuse

Use rechargeable batteries and maintain appliances for longer life spans.

Recycle

Properly dispose of electronic waste and participate in recycling programs.

Recover

Convert waste to energy and implement energy recovery systems.
